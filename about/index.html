About Maddi Anderson

Hello! My name is Maddi Anderson, and I am currently a junior at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. I am studying sports media as well as minoring in business administration. I have always been fascinated with watching sports and storytelling, and found out I can do both of those things through sports journalism. My time at the journalism school is almost up as I graduate in May of 2023, but the things I have learned at my time here so far will last a life time and I cannot wait to use my knowledge in the professional world.
